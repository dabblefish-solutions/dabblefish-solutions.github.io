Speaking with AI

Practice Scenarios

Exercise 1: Email Communication (10 Minutes)

The Scenario:
You sent a proposal to a potential client two weeks ago. They expressed interest in the initial meeting but haven't responded to your follow-up email.
Your Challenge:
Write a follow-up email that re-engages them without seeming desperate.
Structure (10 minutes total):
Minutes 1-2: Apply the WHO-WHY-WHAT-HOW framework to this scenario
Minutes 3-7: Individual work - create your prompt and test it with AI
Minutes 8-10: Partner share - compare approaches and results

Exercise 2: Content Creation (10 Minutes)

The Scenario:
Your company blog needs a post about industry trends. You want to position your business as knowledgeable while providing value to readers.
Your Challenge:
Create an outline for a blog post that demonstrates expertise without being overly promotional.
Structure (10 minutes total):
Minutes 1-2: Build on Exercise 1 - how does content creation differ from email?
Minutes 3-7: Individual work - create your prompt, test, and refine
Minutes 8-10: Group discussion - share what worked and what didn't

Exercise 3: Business Analysis (10 Minutes)

The Scenario:
You have sales data from last quarter showing performance by product line and region. You need to present findings to leadership next week.
Your Challenge:
Ask AI to help analyze patterns and create executive talking points.
Structure (10 minutes total):
Minutes 1-2: Discuss how analysis requests differ from communication tasks
Minutes 3-7: Individual work - create the most sophisticated prompt yet
Minutes 8-10: Whole group - test 2-3 prompts live and compare results
