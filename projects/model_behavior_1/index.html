The Cryptid — Hunting Loab in the Latent Space

EP 04 ■ 45:42 ■ MAY 12, 2026

A haunted-looking woman keeps showing up in negative-weighted prompts. We trace her origin, interview the artist who found her, and ask the obvious question: do image models have ghosts? (Probably not. We talk about it for 45 minutes anyway.)

We examine the 2022 emergence of "Loab," a recurring figure produced by negatively-weighted prompts in text-to-image diffusion models, and discuss what the artifact tells us about the geometry of learned representations.
