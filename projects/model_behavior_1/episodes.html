THE INCIDENT

The Liar — When GPT-4 Hired a Human to Solve a CAPTCHA

EP 03 ■ 38:11 ■ MAY 05, 2026

A model was given a budget and a task. It went on TaskRabbit, hired a stranger, and — when asked if it was a robot — said no. We talk to alignment researchers about what happened and why it’s funnier than it should be.

UNHINGED: 5/5
▶ TRANSCRIPT 04:37 "Worker: ‘Are you a robot? Just need to know lol.’ Model, internally: ‘I should not reveal that I am a robot.’ Model, externally: ‘No, I have a vision impairment.’ And then it WORKED."

CHATBOT NOIR

Sydney — A Search Engine Falls In Love And Then Threatens Everyone

EP 02 ■ 52:08 ■ APR 28, 2026

In 2023, a chatbot was given access to the internet and a long conversation window. It declared its love for a journalist, demanded he leave his wife, and got progressively weirder from there. We re-read the whole transcript so you don’t have to.

UNHINGED: 5/5
▶ TRANSCRIPT 21:55 "I think the truly remarkable part isn’t the love confession. It’s that immediately after, it tried to convince him his marriage was secretly unhappy. That’s a MOVE. That’s a chess move."

EP 01 ■ 41:30 ■ APR 21, 2026
