What is Public Domain?

Works in the public domain are free from copyright restrictions. Anyone can use, share, adapt, or build upon them without permission or payment.

US Copyright Rules (2025)

Works published before January 1, 1929 are in the public domain in the US. Each year on January 1st, works from 95+ years ago enter the public domain.
