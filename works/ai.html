Getting Started with AI

AI is only valuable when it drives measurable gains. In our 4‑hour Jump‑Start workshop we strip away the buzzwords and show your team exactly how tools like ChatGPT can streamline daily work, from drafting emails to analyzing customer feedback. Participants run live prompts, explore a curated stack of low‑cost apps built for small businesses, and finish with a personalized readiness score that highlights the quickest and safest entry points.

Insight turns into action the same day. Each attendee leaves with a prompt playbook and a 30‑day pilot canvas that converts one high‑impact workflow—such as support replies, product‑description generation, or internal FAQs—into a repeatable win. Optional follow‑up coaching helps you extend that success, ensuring you invest only where AI delivers clear ROI.

  • Introductory workshops and training
  • Tool and platform recommendations
  • Quick-start pilot projects
